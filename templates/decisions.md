## How to use

Log a decision whenever:
- A Go/No-Go is called
- A buy vs build choice is made
- A constraint rules out an option
- A stakeholder HITL zone is defined
- A tech stack choice is made (and alternatives were considered)
- A scope item is explicitly excluded
- A tradeoff is presented and a direction is chosen
- An assumption is treated as fact

Format per entry:

```
### [Short decision title]
**Phase:** C / R / I / S
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Decision:** What was decided, in one sentence.
**Why:** The reason — constraint, data point, client input, or tradeoff.
**Alternatives considered:** What else was on the table.
**Source:** Who made the call (client, you, both).
```
