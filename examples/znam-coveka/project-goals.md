# Project Goals

**Project:** Znam Coveka
**Date:** 2026-03-24

---

## Goals

1. Get homeowners a vetted, available tradie — matched and explained — in under 5 minutes of posting a job
2. Get tradies a steady pipeline of real, pre-qualified jobs without bidding or per-lead fees
3. Prove the model in Belgrade (painters + plumbers) before expanding trade types or cities
4. Make the job funnel the health indicator of the business — 20+ jobs posted per week by month 6
5. Build a preference learning loop that makes every match smarter over time without asking users to do extra work

## Non-Goals

- Expanding beyond Belgrade before month 6 traction metrics are hit
- Adding trade types beyond painters and plumbers before the core model is proven
- Building payments / subscription billing at MVP — manual tradie onboarding first
- Building a native mobile app — web-first, responsive
- Fraud detection automation — manual admin at MVP
- B2B / enterprise accounts — post-traction only

## Success Criteria (from Phase R — locked)

| Metric | Target (month 6) |
|---|---|
| Time to shortlist | < 5 minutes |
| Time from posting to job start | < 1 week |
| Active tradies | 50 |
| Jobs posted per week | 20+ |
| Jobs matched | > 80% of posted |
| Jobs completed | > 60% of matched |
| Match acceptance rate | > 60% |
| Tradie subscription retention (month 2+) | > 70% |
| Homeowner repeat usage | > 40% |

## Elicited Goals (beyond the metrics)

**The moment of pride:** A tradie in Belgrade — someone who spent years losing bids to cheaper competitors — sends Mirko a message saying "I haven't had to bid in 3 months and I'm fully booked." That's the moment.

**The unstated goal:** Prove the model works well enough to show it to investors or a co-founder. Belgrade traction is the pitch.

**Conflict check:** Speed of onboarding (homeowner friction = 0) vs quality of matching (data needed to match well). Resolved: collect tradie preferences at onboarding (3 questions max), job preferences at job posting. Never ask homeowners to fill in extra forms outside those natural moments.

## Priority Order (if goals conflict)

1. Job volume — no jobs, no platform
2. Match quality — bad matches destroy trust faster than anything
3. Tradie retention — supply without retention is a leaky bucket
4. Homeowner repeat — demand compounds if the first experience is good
