# Value Proposition Canvas

**Project:** Znam Coveka
**Date:** 2026-03-24

---

## Customer Profile — Homeowner

### Customer Jobs
- Find a reliable painter or plumber for a specific job
- Assess quality before committing (did they do this type of work before? Are they trustworthy?)
- Arrange the job without exchanging personal contact details with strangers
- Get the job done without chasing, rescheduling, or being ghosted

### Pains
- Can't tell a good tradie from a bad one until it's too late (money lost, bad work)
- Scrolling through directories and making 5 calls to get 1 response is exhausting
- Word of mouth is unreliable and doesn't scale (your friend's plumber is booked for 3 weeks)
- Getting 6 quotes and not knowing which one to trust or why
- Fear of being overcharged because they don't know the going rate

### Gains
- Confidence that the tradie showing up is vetted and right for the job
- Speed — choose from 3 pre-filtered options, done in minutes
- Understand why each tradie was recommended for their specific job
- No haggling, no fake quotes, no wasted calls

---

## Customer Profile — Tradie (Painter / Plumber)

### Customer Jobs
- Get a steady pipeline of real jobs without spending hours pitching
- Build a reputation that earns them more work without marketing spend
- Run their business without depending on word of mouth or platforms that take them for a ride

### Pains
- Writing bids that go nowhere (time wasted, dignity cost)
- Losing jobs to cheaper competitors even when their work is better
- Paying per lead on Bark/Thumbtack and getting fake phone numbers back
- Unpredictable income — feast or famine, no smoothed pipeline
- No way to showcase quality — all platforms reduce them to a star rating

### Gains
- Pre-qualified jobs land in their pipeline — no bidding, no chasing
- Pay one subscription fee, get real access (not per-lead roulette)
- Their quality is surfaced to homeowners through vetting and AI explanation
- Predictable, sustainable workload

## Value Map

### Products & Services
- Job posting flow for homeowners (job type, description, location, photos)
- Tradie vetting and profile (portfolio, past work photos, trade verification, ratings)
- AI-powered matching engine (trade type, location, availability, rating, job fit)
- AI-generated match explanation (why this tradie for this specific job)
- 2-3 curated shortlist presented to homeowner
- Homeowner selects → tradie notified → job confirmed
- In-platform messaging (no personal contact exchange)
- Tradie subscription access model

### Pain Relievers
- Homeowner gets 3 pre-selected options with explanations — no scrolling, no cold calls
- AI explanation removes the "why should I trust this person?" anxiety
- Tradies never bid — they either match or they don't (no race to the bottom)
- No per-lead fees — one subscription, real pipeline
- Vetting process ensures only quality tradies appear in shortlists

### Gain Creators
- Homeowners make a confident decision in minutes, not days
- Tradies build reputation through the platform's quality signal, not just word of mouth
- Both sides experience reliability that the current market completely fails to provide
- Tradies with strong track records get surfaced more — quality is rewarded, not cheapness
