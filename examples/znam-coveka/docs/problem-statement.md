## Business Context
_(Mirrored back and confirmed by client)_

Znam Coveka connects two groups who both have a trust and reliability problem: tradies who waste time chasing leads and bidding against each other, and homeowners who can't tell a reliable tradie from a bad one until it's too late. The platform removes the bidding game — tradies get matched to jobs, homeowners get vetted tradies with an AI-generated explanation of why each one fits their specific job. The tradie pays for access; the homeowner pays nothing.

Launch market: Belgrade, Serbia. Starting with painters and plumbers.
