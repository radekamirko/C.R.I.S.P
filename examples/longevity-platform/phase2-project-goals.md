# Project Goals

**Project:** Longevity Intelligence Platform
**Date:** 2026-03-23
**Linked to:** phase1-problem-statement.md + phase2-stakeholder-register.md

---

## Goals

- [x] Replace the fragmented 4-5 app daily workflow with a single session under 2 minutes
- [x] Eliminate manual context re-entry — the platform remembers the user across sessions
- [x] Deliver contextually aware training suggestions (HRV, training load, weather, air quality)
- [x] Enable photo-based nutrition logging with inflammation and glycemic spike insight
- [x] Surface the connection between food and training/recovery in one unified view

---

## Non-Goals (POC scope)

- Garmin workout export / direct sync — Phase 2
- Apple Watch write-back — Phase 2
- Social or community features
- Clinical integrations or medical records
- Prescriptive health advice — never (product positioning)

---

## Success Criteria Summary

> This project succeeds when:
> 1. A user completes their daily training + nutrition loop in under 2 minutes with zero manual context re-entry
> 2. Training suggestions feel contextually aware — they reflect the user's actual recovery, environment, and goal without prompting
> 3. Food logging is faster and more insightful than LoseIt — photo snap works, and inflammation/glycemic signal is present
