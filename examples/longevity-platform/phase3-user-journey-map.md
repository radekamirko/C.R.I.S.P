# User Journey Map

**Project:** Longevity Intelligence Platform
**User type:** Health-conscious adult, late 30s–50s, wearable user, longevity-motivated
**Date:** 2026-03-23

---

## Journey: Evening Training Planning

| Step | User action | Needs | Feelings | Thoughts | Pain points |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Opens platform | See today's recovery status without effort | Tired, winding down | "How did I recover today?" | None — data is already there |
| 2 | Views HRV, sleep, load summary | Understand if body is ready | Curious | "Looks like I recovered well" | None |
| 3 | Reads training suggestion with reasoning | Trust in the suggestion | Reassured or skeptical | "Does this make sense given the weather?" | None — weather/AQI already factored in |
| 4 | Tweaks or confirms workout | Control over the decision | In control | "Yes, that works for tomorrow" | Minimal — one tap to confirm |
| 5 | Workout saved | Done, ready for tomorrow | Satisfied | "That took 90 seconds" | None |

## Journey: Nutrition Logging

| Step | User action | Needs | Feelings | Thoughts | Pain points |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Snaps photo of meal | Fast, effortless logging | Slightly reluctant (habit break from LoseIt) | "Let's see if this actually works" | First-time trust gap |
| 2 | Reviews and confirms food items | Accuracy | Engaged | "It got the salmon right, missed the sauce" | Occasional misidentification — correctable |
| 3 | Reads inflammation / glycemic insight | Understand food impact | Surprised or validated | "I didn't realise that was high GI" | None |
| 4 | Sees nudge connecting meal to tomorrow's training | Holistic view | Delighted | "It's telling me this might affect my run tomorrow" | None — this is the moment of delight |

## Journey: Weekly Review

| Step | User action | Needs | Feelings | Thoughts | Pain points |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Opens weekly digest | See progress, not just data | Motivated | "Did I move the needle this week?" | None |
| 2 | Reviews 4 sub-scores (fitness, nutrition, recovery, environment) | Understand where to focus | Reflective | "Nutrition dragged my score — makes sense, I ate badly Thursday" | None |
| 3 | Reads trend insights | Actionable patterns | Curious | "My HRV drops when AQI is above 80 — I never noticed that" | None |
| 4 | Gets monthly baseline re-test prompt | Track longevity progress over time | Motivated | "Time to check my grip strength again" | Slight friction — requires physical test |

---

## Key Insights

- The user's primary emotion in the current workflow is **friction and resignation** — they do it because it's the best available option, not because it's good
- The moment of highest potential delight is the **food ↔ training connection** — it's invisible today and would feel like a revelation
- Trust in the platform will be built through **accuracy of food recognition** and **quality of first training suggestion** — get these right in onboarding
- The weekly score must feel like a **mirror, not a judge** — language and framing matter enormously here

## Moments of Delight

- First evening: suggestion already knows HRV, AQI, and weather — user doesn't have to say anything
- First nutrition snap: photo recognised correctly in seconds
- First time platform says "your inflammation markers were high this week — likely correlated with Thursday's meals — here's what the science says"
- First weekly score that shows an upward trend — user sees they're moving

## Biggest Pain Points (current, eliminated by platform)

- Re-entering goal and context every single session
- Fragmented app switching before getting a single insight
- Training suggestions that ignore environment (weather, AQI)
- Manual food logging with no insight beyond calories
- No visible connection between what you eat and how you recover or perform
