# Problem Statement

**Project:** Longevity Intelligence Platform
**Date:** 2026-03-23
**Client:** Mirko Radeka (bootstrapped)

---

## One Sentence

> Currently, health-conscious people in their late 30s+ are spending hours fragmented across multiple AI chats and apps — getting answers without context, and losing insights over time — because no single tool connects their personal biometric data to longevity science with long-term memory.

**Agreed statement:** Yes — confirmed by client.

---

## Business Context

A B2C longevity intelligence platform that curates and surfaces the latest scientifically backed data on quality of life and lifespan — presented in a way that's accessible to everyday people, without prescribing or advising. The user is in the driver's seat.

The platform is personalized: it contextualizes science to the user's actual biometric data (HRV, VO2max, sleep, nutrition, social connections) via Garmin and Apple Watch integrations. It holds long-term memory of the user's data — something no current fragmented chat workflow can do.

It is not a health coach. It does not prescribe or advise. It shows data and science; the user decides.

## Target User
